Academic

Anonymous Reporting & Student Wellbeing System

Degree final project — a platform enabling students to submit anonymous reports on wellbeing concerns, misconduct, or campus issues. Designed with privacy, usability, and institutional safety in mind.

Degree Project System Design Privacy HCI
2024 +
Built as the culmination of the BSc Information Science programme, this system focused on giving students a safe, low-friction way to flag concerns without fear of exposure. Key considerations included anonymised submission handling, role-based access for administrators reviewing reports, and a UI designed to feel approachable rather than bureaucratic. The project drew on coursework in HCI, Information Systems Security & Audit, and systems design to balance usability with institutional trust and data protection.
02

Restaurant Management System

Diploma final project — a full management system for restaurant operations covering order management, inventory tracking, and billing. Built to streamline day-to-day workflows for small food businesses.

Diploma Project System Design Database Operations
2022 +
Developed as the final diploma project at JKUAT, this system modeled the core operations of a small restaurant end to end — from taking and tracking orders, to managing stock levels, to generating bills. The build emphasised a relational database structure to keep orders, inventory, and billing in sync, along with a straightforward interface aimed at staff with limited technical background. It was a hands-on introduction to translating a real business workflow into working software.
